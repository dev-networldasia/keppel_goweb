Unique architecture
and design

The development foreground provides idyllic, and porosity knitted intimately with the city wider fabric.

The blurring of the boundary between the development and the city provides essential and easy discovery of the new development within the old city - an urban oasis truly.

The design driving principle is to pay homage to the heritage site celebrating the old-world charm of the old city of Hanoi.

The mall's unique retail space harmoniously blend nature with shopping, with the vision to honor the city's rich history and cultural heritage by preserving an iconic banyan tree within the mall. This innovative approach was recently crowned "Best Retail Interior Design" at the 10th PropertyGuru Vietnam Property Awards.

Central Square

A connecting space between the 3 blocks, blending indoor with outdoor activities. The Central Square will have lush green landscaped spaces dotted with clusters of trees providing shade and refreshing green spaces, where activities come alive, and the flavors of Hanoi unfold amidst a captivating atmosphere.

In addition, this space is envisioned to be activated as an extension of the indoor space, likely to host a variety of community events or gatherings such as thematic fairs, exhibitions, forums, activity centers, etc.
